Foundational Pillars

One of the most dramatic architectural features of our building is the library, with its soaring ceilings and four prominent pillars. It is appropriate that the vision of success for our school is also built upon four foundational pillars! They are:

Passion

Find what drives you to do more, be more, achieve more

Resilience

The capacity to persevere when faced with challenges

Community

Share your gifts with those around you

Character

Doing the right thing when no one is looking

PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

Welcome to Lillian Osborne High School, Lillian Osborne High School is a vibrant, innovative and active community of learners committed to fostering personal excellence in the areas of Academics, Athletics and the Arts. Providing the opportunity for every student to realize his or her potential in a positive learning environment, and to achieve academic excellence in the pursuit of their post-secondary studies and career pathway, is our goal. We set high standards for academic achievement and the staff at Lillian Osborne is committed to providing the knowledge, skills, and support necessary to ensure the success of all students. Our School Mission is "Inspiring our students through meaningful learning opportunities that nurture community, resilience, passion, academic success and good character.‚Äù The relationships our students, staff and community forge, guide us in our journey toward engaged life-long learning and active global citizenship.
